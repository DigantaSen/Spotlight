How to Play

The Stoplight Game is a strategic game where two players simultaneously choose one of two actions: Go (Green) or Stop (Red). The payoffs depend on both players' choices, creating interesting strategic interactions that demonstrate Nash Equilibrium concepts.

Nash Equilibrium Explanation

A Nash Equilibrium occurs when no player can improve their payoff by unilaterally changing their strategy, given the other player's strategy. In this game, we analyze each outcome to determine if it represents a Nash Equilibrium.
